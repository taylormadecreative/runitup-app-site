Before you pull up

Good to know

Does it cost anything?

No. Run It UP! Dallas is free — no fees, no memberships. Show up, run, hang out.

Do I need to be fast?

Not even a little. There are four pace groups from a 16-minute-mile walk to a sub-8 sprint, and every group has a crew. Nobody gets left behind.

What do I bring to my first run?

Shoes, water, and the app if you want your first run to count toward your streak. Come a few minutes early and say you're new — the crew will take it from there.

Do I need the app to run with the club?

No — just pull up. The app is where check-ins, streaks, badges, run buddies, and the crew chat live, and you can explore it as a guest first.

Is it Android-friendly?

The app is on iPhone today. Android runners are still crew — the schedule on this page is always current.
